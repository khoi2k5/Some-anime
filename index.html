Some Good And Impressive Animes

I like watching anime, which is interesting and inspiring. Anime gives us a better understanding of Japanese culture. Here are some anime I recommend for you:

  • Your name
  • Darling in the Franxx
  • Your Lie in April

Your name

  • A work by Shinkai Makoto, Your Name has brought many tears to the audience.
  • This is an anime about the fantasy and romance genre.
  • This is a story about the love story of two characters Taki and Mitsuha.
  • Your Name is an open ending movie that allows viewers to imagine for themselves.

Darling in the Franxx

  • The story begins in a desolate world where humans must live in mobile castles and fight monsters with machines called Franxx.
  • The condition to be able to control the Franxx is to have 2 children and it must be both male and female.
  • The main character Hiro is a smart guy but can't drive a Franxx until he meets Zero Two.
  • All of Zero Two's previous partners were in danger while piloting the franxx with her, but Hiro still defied it to be able to pilot the Franxx.
  • So how will the love story of high school age unfold? Please watch Darling in the Franxx.

Picture of Darling in the Franxx src

Your Lie in April

  • Kousei Arima is a piano genius who is known for his perfect piano playing ability. After his mother passed away, Arima could not hear the melody when playing the piano. Kaori Miyazono is a girl with a unique personality who plays the violin not according to music score but according to her own feelings.
  • Unexpectedly meeting a girl who is completely opposite to his personality helps Arima solve his psychological problems and return to playing the piano.
  • This is an extremely interesting anime about school love, watch Your Lie in April to feel the passion and love of youth.
